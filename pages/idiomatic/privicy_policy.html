Privacy Policy - Idiomatic

At Idiomatic, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our app. Please read this policy carefully.

Idiomatic does not collect, store, or process any personal data or identifiable information from users.

We do not store or retain any data submitted for translation. Once the translation is completed, the data is not kept on our servers, and we do not track or log any personally identifiable information.

Use of Third-Party Services

To provide translation services within the app, Idiomatic utilizes the LibreTranslate models, which are downloaded and hosted locally on our own servers. This means that when you use the translation feature, your data (such as text entered for translation) is processed directly on our infrastructure, not on any external server.

We do not store or retain any data submitted for translation. Once the translation is completed, the data is not kept on our servers, and we do not track or log any personally identifiable information.
